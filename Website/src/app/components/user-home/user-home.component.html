About Task Ace

Task Ace helps you conquer your to-do list with a focus on organization and clarity. It offers multiple views to prioritize tasks, like "Today" to see your daily agenda or "Upcoming" for a bird's-eye view of your week or month. You can structure your tasks with projects and subprojects, making it easy to manage complex workflows. Task Ace also allows setting deadlines and reminders, assigning labels for further filtering, and collaborating with others on shared projects.

Task Ace boasts features to enhance your productivity. You can capture tasks with voice commands or quickly add them from anywhere using shortcuts. Location-based reminders ensure you don't forget errands when you're out, and karma points gamify the process, keeping you motivated.

How it works

Task Ace allows undoing mistakes.Click on the circle next to the task to mark it incomplete again. Alternatively, right-click on the task and select "Mark Incomplete" from the menu.

The feature of tracking the progress is not being implemented yet. Our team will definetly add this feature and let you know about it. Meanwhile, You can now change the progress of the task.

Task Ace offers different ways to prioritize tasks: Use priority levels: Click on the star icon next to a task and choose a priority level (1-4). Higher numbers indicate higher importance. Drag and drop tasks: Reorder your task list by dragging and dropping tasks to change their priority visually.
